SK_FoodTXR_WebApp

Hello, Thank you for visting the site.

This is a trial site to help users (target audience: patients and their family) identify drinks and foods suitable for their current swallowing ability and to ensure safe oral intake.

If you follow the selection criteria closely, it determines whether your item of interest meets the recommended International Dysphagia Diet Standardisation Initiative (IDDSI) level. It will also provide an approximated IDDSI level matched to your item of interest, if you do not know your IDDSI level.

Currently, this web app does not have detailed recommendations for modification techniques. It is a functionality that can be added later.

For more detailed classification of IDDSI levels, please see the reference tab above linked to the IDDSI Framework. Remember to always consult with your doctor and speech-language pathologist to confirm your current oral intake/IDDSI levels.

Click on the Test App button below or at the top to begin exploring the program. Please use the built-in buttons to navigate between pages instead of the browser forward and backward buttons. If you need to go back and correct a selection choice, it is best to start again by using the "Back To Start" button. Current program works best in a desktop environment, and is compatible with the Chrome and Microsoft Edge browsers.
