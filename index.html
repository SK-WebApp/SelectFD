SK_FoodTXR_WebApp_1.0.1

Hello, thank you for visiting the site.

This is a trial web app to help users (target audience: swallowing patients and their family members) more easily identify drinks and foods suitable for patients' current swallowing ability to ensure safe oral intake.

If you follow the selection criteria closely, it will help narrow down the International Dysphagia Diet Standardisation Initiative (IDDSI) levels and determine whether your item of interest meets your recommended IDDSI level. It will also provide an approximate IDDSI level matched to the item of interest if you do not have assigned IDDSI levels.

This web app serves as an initial screening tool, a potential first module in a series of interconnected modules to help address patients' comprehensive swallowing needs. Other modules or functionalities such as Food-Drink Modification Techniques and a more stringent Clinical IDDSI Levels Verification Tests can be added later.

For more detailed classification of IDDSI levels, please see the reference tab above linked to the IDDSI Framework. Remember to always consult with your doctor and speech-language pathologist to confirm your current oral intake/IDDSI levels.

Notes on Web App Navigation:
Click on the Test App button below or at the top to begin exploring the application. Please use the built-in buttons to navigate between pages instead of the browser's forward and back arrow buttons. If you need to go back and correct a selection choice, please start again by using the "Back To Start" button instead of the browser back arrow. Current program works best in a desktop environment, and is compatible with the Chrome, Safari and Microsoft Edge browsers.
