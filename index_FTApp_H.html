This is a trial site for users to help identify food texture and drink thickness suitable for their current diet and to ensure safe swallowing. To begin, please enter the following information.

Step1: Enter the item you want to eat or drink, one item per entry and keep the description simple (e.g., chicken nuggets, apple juice).

Step2: Select the IDDSI levels your clinician has assigned you. There are typically two assigned levels: one for drinks and one for foods. If you do not know your IDDSI levels, just select "Do Not Know".
